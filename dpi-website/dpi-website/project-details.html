The Challenge

Understanding the Problem

The client's existing e-commerce platform was suffering from several critical issues that were impacting their business performance:

  • Outdated design that didn't reflect the brand's premium positioning
  • Poor mobile experience with low conversion rates on smartphones
  • Slow page load times leading to high bounce rates
  • Complex checkout process causing cart abandonment
  • Lack of personalization and product recommendations

Our challenge was to address these issues while ensuring a seamless transition for existing customers and maintaining SEO rankings.

Our Approach

The Solution

01

User Research & Discovery

We conducted in-depth user research, including surveys, interviews, and usability testing to understand customer pain points and behaviors. This helped us create detailed user personas and customer journey maps.

02

Information Architecture

We restructured the site's navigation and content hierarchy to make it more intuitive. The new sitemap reduced the number of clicks needed to reach key pages and improved overall findability.

03

UI/UX Design

Our design team created a modern, clean interface that aligned with the brand's identity. We focused on creating a visual hierarchy that guides users through the shopping journey with clear calls-to-action.

04

Development & Testing

Using React and Node.js, we built a performant, responsive frontend with a focus on speed and accessibility. Rigorous testing was conducted across devices and browsers to ensure a consistent experience.
